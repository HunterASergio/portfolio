Danilo

Danilo is a co-owner of D&A Juices. Leaving high school, he was unsure what he wanted to do as far as a career, but he always wanted to have his own business. Eventually he decided to go to school for business management. During that time in school, him and Alejandra were thinking of ideas to start a business. Well, somehow after making homemade cold pressed juices one night in their kitchen, they ended up creating D&A Juices together after hearing all the positive feedback they received from their close friends and family! Now, Danilo works hard each day with Alejandra to create delicious products for the community. Soon, he will begin to expand their business to reach a wider audience so that eveyone can experience D&A Juices!
