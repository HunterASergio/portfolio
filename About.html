OUR COMPANY

With fast food restaurants around every corner, advertisements on television for calorie dense food, and social media displaying all the amazing food the world has to offer, it can be really difficult trying to make an effort to eat healthy. That is why we created D&A Juices. We want to offer our community a friendly environment where you can feel good about what you're eating. From our cold pressed juices to our acai bowls, our products are all tasty and healthy. We make sure that each day we walk into our facility, we have fresh fruits and vegetables available to use. If any of our products are not up to standard, we will not use or serve it as we very much value the quality of our products. As far as our goals for this company, we have a few that we want to reach our community:

Goal One:
- We want our customers to improve the way they think about health. This includes their body, nutrition, and mindset. Everyone wants to look and feel good, but it has to start from somewhere. You can workout as much as you want, but if you don't change the things that you put in your body, it will be very hard to acheive the goals you set for yourself. Even if you only come to our location once to get started in your journey to better health, we appreciate that you start with us.
Goal Two:
- We want to be as transparent as possible. We can't be helpful to our community if we can't show or tell you exactly how we operate. This is why our location has an open and visible prep area so you can see exactly what we prepare each day, every order. We also encourage our customers to ask questions. We want you to be certain in what you're spending your hard earned money on.
